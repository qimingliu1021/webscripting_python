## About
Webscript data from alibaba.com, focused on apparel manufactures. 

## Key aspects we focused on for manufactures

### 1. \*Production Capacity

**Industry Reports**: Look for industry publications that discuss production capacity trends or benchmarks.
**Import/Export Data**: Analyze import/export data to estimate production volume by tracking shipments and trade activity.

### 2. \*Speed of Production and Delivery

**Shipping and Logistics Reports**: Public data on shipping times and logistics networks can provide insights into delivery speed.
**Industry Benchmarks**: Industry reports often contain aggregated lead times for various product types and regions.

### 3. \*Minimum Order Size

**Manufacturer Websites**: Often list minimum order requirements directly.
**Industry Forums and Marketplaces**: Online B2B marketplaces or forums frequently share order size information for manufacturers.

### 4. \*Product Range and Specialization

**Manufacturer Websites and Catalogs**: Review online catalogs to understand product ranges.
**Industry Publications and Marketplaces**: Aggregated data often highlights specialization trends.

### 5. \*Sustainability Practices

**Certifications and Standards**: Search databases for certifications (e.g., Fair Trade, GOTS) or compliance with environmental standards.
**Corporate Social Responsibility Reports**: Many manufacturers publicly share their sustainability practices.

### 6. \*Ethical Standards

**Certification and NGO Databases**: Organizations like B Corp and Fair Wear Foundation publish lists of ethically certified manufacturers.
**Social Compliance Reports**: Government agencies and NGOs often release reports on labor practices.

### 7. \*Product Quality and Reviews

**E-commerce Platforms**: Aggregate product reviews from popular platforms (e.g., Amazon, Alibaba).
**Consumer Review Sites**: Check specialized consumer review sites like Trustpilot.

### 8. \*Popularity and Brand Perception

**Social Media Analysis**: Use social listening tools to analyze brand sentiment on platforms like Twitter or Instagram.
**Search Trend Data**: Public search trend data (e.g., Google Trends) can indicate brand popularity.

### 9. \*Pricing Structure

**E-commerce Platforms and Marketplaces**: Aggregate pricing information from platforms like Alibaba or industry-specific marketplaces.
**Market Reports**: Pricing trends are often available in market analysis reports.

### 10. \*Geographical Location

**Company Registries and Directories**: National business registries often contain location data.
**Trade Databases**: Public customs or trade databases frequently provide geographical shipment information.
